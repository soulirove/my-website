Not Just an Ordinary
Love Story

We met in the Autumn of 2018 in Debrecen, a small, but charming, Hungarian city. One of us was doing Erasmus and the other was there studying full time. Our time together was filled with unforgettable moments, and we knew that our love was something special. When it was time to part ways, we faced the challenge of being apart. Over the next four years, we nurtured our love across thousands of kilometers and met every few months in different cities. We held onto the dream of reuniting and, in 2022, that dream came true when we moved to Porto and asked each other to be together forever. As we now prepare to say "I do," we are filled with joy and gratitude, ready to celebrate with you the beautiful journey that has brought us to this moment.
